What?

The Blockchain is a decentralized and distributed digital ledger. In the same way the internet has facilitated direct exchange of information, the blockchain facilitates direct exchange of value between parties, without the need for a trusted intermediary. Most known for its role as the underlying technology behind Bitcoin, it is slowly revolutionizing our modern society.

About

Badger Blockchain is the first official UW student blockchain organization, bringing together students and the larger Madison community to study blockchain technologies. Our team focuses on the present and future reality of the environment, which includes development of smart contracts and dApps, analysis of crypto-markets and protocols, and research into various industrial use-cases and impending legal regulations.

Furthermore, as an affiliate of BEN (the Blockchain Education Network), Badger Blockchain provides a lasting platform for students, educators, entrepreneurs, community leaders, and respected businesses within the field to collaborate and build an ever more active blockchain community.
